Shetland yarns palette picker

This color palette shows Spindrift and Double Knitting yarn colors from Jamieson's of Shetland. This tool is not affiliated with Jamieson's in any way.

How to use this tool

  • Add yarns to your "selected colors" by clicking on them in the palette
  • Drag your selected colors to change their order
  • Hover over a yarn and click the "remove" button to remove it from the selected colors
